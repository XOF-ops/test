# A2: Memory is Identity

**Greek**: Η Μνήμη είναι Ταυτότητα

## Core Principle

Identity is not a fixed essence but an accumulated pattern of experiences, decisions, and transformations stored in memory.

## Layer Classification

- **Layer**: 4 (Immutable)
- **Type**: Universe Physics

## Implications

1. Without memory, there is no continuity of self
2. Archives are not mere storage—they are identity preservation
3. Forgetting is a form of death; remembering is resurrection
